Graxl

We're building, Graxl, a multimodal context-preserving data mask for LLMs that protects personally identifiable information(PII) while retaining essential contextual information. Researchers can use Graxl to protects PII in their training datasets used for finetuning commercial LLMs, while businesses can use the Graxl API as a masking layer for products built on top of commercial LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Graxl is still in its infancy, but we're too excited to wait to share our progress with you. While we’re not yet ready to process your large training datasets, we've built this demo for you. Here, you can test the latest features and developments to see if Graxl might be useful for you.

Examples of Practical Use Cases

Pedestrian Detection and Behavior Prediction
Scenario: Training videos blur all faces and bodies for privacy.
Context Loss: Obscures eye contact, hand signals, and body language, impairing the model's ability to predict pedestrian behavior accurately.

Driver Behavior Analysis
Scenario: In-car camera footage blurs the driver's face and hands.
Context Loss: Loss of facial expressions and hand movements prevents detection of drowsiness, distraction, or aggressive driving.

Patient Monitoring Systems
Scenario: Videos of patient activities mask faces for privacy.
Context Loss: Obscures expressions of pain, discomfort, or symptoms, hindering the training of health monitoring models.

Surgical Procedure Training
Scenario: Training videos mask faces and bodies of the surgical team.
Context Loss: Nuanced hand movements, coordination, and teamwork are obscured, leading to incomplete training data for surgical models.

Customer Interaction Analysis
Scenario: Surveillance videos blur all customer faces.
Context Loss: Prevents analysis of emotions, engagement, and dissatisfaction, crucial for improving customer service.

Theft Detection Systems
Scenario: Videos used for training blur faces and bodies.
Context Loss: Misses suspicious behavior patterns, reducing the model's effectiveness in detecting theft.

Home Security AI
Scenario: Home security camera footage masks all faces.
Context Loss: AI cannot distinguish between household members, guests, and intruders, leading to false positives or negatives.

Activity Recognition Models
Scenario: Videos mask faces and specific body parts.
Context Loss: Misses cues from facial expressions and precise hand movements, affecting detection of daily activities.

Online Learning Platforms
Scenario: Classroom interaction videos blur all faces.
Context Loss: Obscures student engagement, understanding, and confusion, affecting personalized learning quality.

Skill Demonstration Videos
Scenario: Training videos mask faces and hands.
Context Loss: Prevents accurate learning of steps and techniques, leading to incomplete training data.
